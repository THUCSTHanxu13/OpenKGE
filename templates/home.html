We are OpenKGE

OpenKGE is open knowledge graph embeddings and toolkits organized by THUNLP. We provide an extremely fast implementation of some existing knowledge representation learning (KRL) methods. Besides some underlying design changes for acceleration, our implementation also supports multi-threaded training to save time. We also provide embeddings of the existing large-scale knowledge graphs learned by our toolkits, which can be directly used for some research fields.
